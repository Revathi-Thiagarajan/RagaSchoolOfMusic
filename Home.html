Welcome to Raga School of Music

Our Mission

At Raga School of Music, we are dedicated to providing high-quality music education to students of all ages and skill levels. Our experienced and passionate instructors are committed to helping each student achieve their musical goals.

Our Programs

We at Raga School of Music are committed to developing students' musical ability and helping them develop a profound respect for the rich history of ragas. Our journey started with the goal of establishing a thriving, exciting, and immersive community of music fans for learning.
Our Collective:
Get to know our devoted group of knowledgeable musicians and instructors that are eager to help you along your musical path. Our wide range of experience, from traditional to modern, guarantees a thorough educational process.
Mission:
We want to give aspiring musicians a place to study, practice, and become experts at ragas. Our belief is that music has the ability to unite individuals from all backgrounds and break down barriers.
Courses:
Explore our selection of classes tailored for students ranging from novices to experts. We offer a program that will suit your interests, whether you are enthusiastic about fusion genres or classical compositions.
